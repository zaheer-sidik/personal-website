Sprint Training Time Predictor - Winter 2025

Skills Developed: JavaScript, HTML, Web Design

Cultured Chicken Production Group Research Project - 2024 to 2025

View the Report
I worked as part of a group to design a plant with the capability of producing 1 tonne of meat per year. I specifically worked on the differentiation stage, focusing on the bioreactor design for the myogenesis and adipogenesis processes. I used ASPEN Plus to model the heat generated within these bioreactors and then used this to design the reactor thickness and jacket sizing. Furthermore, I also worked on the overall material and energy balance for the entire plant. We were also tasked with looking at the financial viability of the plant. To do this I used the overall material and energy balance to come up with the overall CapEx and OpEx. I also performed an optimisation to determine where the plant should be situated and considered supply chain strategy, which involved looking at key suppliers, sensitivity to supply chain disruption and which relationships to prioritise.
Skills Developed: ASPEN Plus, Python - Heat Transfer Modelling

Sustainable Computing - Winter 2024

View the Report
I worked on a project that would take carbon intensity data from the UK and use models to predict future emissions, on the hourly, daily, monthly and yearly timescale. In this project I used Facebook's Prophet model as well as LightGBM. I also used PowerTOP to measure the power consumption of two different laptops when running these models. The findings of this project were used to inform users when the best time to run computationally intensive tasks would be in order to minimise carbon emissions.
Skills Developed: Time Series Forecasting, Python - Data Analysis, Bash/Linux

Liquid Nitrogen Heatsink CFD - Summer 2024

During my internship at the University of Oxford, I worked on a computational fluid dynamics (CFD) project focused on optimising a liquid nitrogen heatsink for power electronics applications. This involved creating the heatsink geometry in SolidWorks, and then simulating the fluid flow and heat transfer in ANSYS Fluent. The goal was to inform the purchasing of a heatsink for experimental testing, through parametric studies and optimisations.
Skills Developed: CFD Simulation, ANSYS Fluent, SolidWorks
